Additional Services

Notifications and Alerts

Push notifications and alerts to keep users informed about important updates, such as new property listings, price changes, or upcoming appointments.

User Authentication and Authorization

Secure login and registration system allowing different user roles (e.g., admin, agent, client) with varying levels of access to the app's features.

Data Backup and Recovery

Regular data backups and a recovery system to protect against data loss, ensuring that all property listings and user information are securely stored.

Customer Support and Chat

A live chat feature or a support ticket system to assist users with any inquiries or issues they may encounter while using the app.

Customizable Property Listings

Agents can customize property listings with multimedia content like photos, videos, floor plans, and detailed descriptions. This service enhances the presentation of properties, making them more attractive to potential clients.

Property Analytics and Reporting

Agents can access detailed analytics and reports on property views, inquiries, and market trends. This data-driven insight helps them make informed decisions and tailor their marketing strategies for better outcomes.
