Agricultural Properties

Kitengela

Ksh 1 million to Ksh 3 million per acre.
Kajiado County, south of Nairobi. Kitengela is a semi-arid area but has fertile soil suitable for livestock farming (cattle, goats, sheep), and horticulture in irrigated areas. It's also close to Nairobi, making it popular for peri-urban farming.

Nyandarua

Ksh 800,000 and Ksh 2 million per acre.
Nyandarua County, in central Kenya near the Aberdare Ranges.
This region has fertile soils and a cool climate ideal for dairy farming, potato farming, and horticulture. It's known for large-scale vegetable production such as carrots, cabbages, and peas.

Timau

Ksh 1.5 million to Ksh 5 million per acre.
Meru County, on the slopes of Mount Kenya.
Timau has fertile volcanic soil and is well-known for large-scale farming, especially wheat, barley, and floriculture. The high altitude provides a favorable climate for horticulture and dairy farming.

Nakuru

Ksh 1 million to Ksh 3.5 million per acre.
Nakuru County, part of the Rift Valley region.
Nakuru has fertile soils and is well-suited for dairy farming, maize, wheat, and flower farming. The area also benefits from proximity to local markets and infrastructure.

Mwea

Ksh 1.2 million to Ksh 4 million per acre.
Kirinyaga County, along the Nairobi-Embu highway.
Mwea is known for rice farming, and irrigation schemes from the Mwea irrigation project make it highly productive for other crops such as vegetables and horticulture.

Coastal Properties

Vipingo Ridge

Ksh 20 million to Ksh 80 million
Kilifi, near Mombasa
Vipingo Ridge is a premier gated community located in Kilifi, near Mombasa, known for its luxury villas and townhouses. The development features an 18-hole championship golf course and offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean, along with exclusive access to private beaches. It combines high-end living with serene surroundings, and is conveniently close to the Vipingo Airstrip for easy travel.

Nyali Beachfront Villas

Ksh 45 million to Ksh 150 million
Nyali, Mombasa
Nyali Beachfront Villas are upscale residences situated along the beautiful Nyali Beach in Mombasa. These villas offer direct beach access, stunning ocean views, and high-end amenities, making them ideal for luxury living or vacationing. The area is well-connected to shopping malls, international schools, and entertainment options, providing both comfort and convenience in a prime coastal location.

Diani Beach Apartments

Ksh 15 million to Ksh 60 million
Diani Beach, Kwale County
Diani Beach Apartments are located along the stunning Diani Beach, known for its white sandy shores and clear blue waters. These modern apartments offer direct access to the beach and are close to luxury resorts, golf courses, and dining options. They are popular for both vacation rentals and private residences, providing a perfect blend of comfort and convenience in a vibrant holiday destination.

Kilifi Creek Residences

Ksh 25 million to Ksh 90 million
Kilifi Creek, Kilifi County
Kilifi Creek Residences are situated along the picturesque Kilifi Creek, offering a serene and tranquil environment. The properties feature a blend of contemporary and Swahili architectural styles, providing private homes with boat access to the creek. The area is close to Kilifi Town, offering convenient access to local amenities and medical facilities, making it ideal for those seeking a peaceful yet connected lifestyle.

Lamu Island Heritage Homes

Ksh 10 million to Ksh 50 million
Lamu Island, Lamu County
Lamu Island Heritage Homes are traditional Swahili-style residences located in the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Lamu Old Town. These homes feature a blend of Arabic and African architectural influences, offering a unique cultural experience in a peaceful, car-free environment. They are ideal for those seeking historical charm and tranquility, with easy access to cultural landmarks and pristine beaches.

Property Advice

Accurate Property Descriptions

Provide detailed and honest descriptions of properties, highlighting unique features. Accurate information builds trust with potential buyers and helps set realistic expectations.

Clear Pricing Information

List clear and competitive pricing, including all costs such as taxes, maintenance fees, or utilities. Transparent pricing helps avoid confusion and makes your listings more appealing.

Update Listings Regularly

Keep listings up-to-date with current availability, price changes, and new information. Regular updates show that your property management is active and attentive, increasing credibility.

Include Floor Plans

Sharing floor plans gives prospective buyers a clearer understanding of the property layout, helping them visualize how they would utilize the space. This can lead to more serious inquiries.

Offer Detailed Neighborhood Insights

Provide an overview of the neighborhood's vibe, safety, and growth potential. Buyers want to know if the area aligns with their lifestyle and long-term plans, so showcasing this adds value to your listing.
