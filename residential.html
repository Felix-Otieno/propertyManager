Residential Properties

Runda

KES 90 million
Runda Estate, nestled in Nairobi's Gigiri suburb, is renowned for its luxurious residential properties set amidst lush greenery. The estate offers a tranquil retreat with spacious villas and mansions, often featuring large gardens and private pools. Its prime location near diplomatic missions and international organizations makes it highly desirable among diplomats, expatriates, and affluent Kenyans.

Muthaiga

KES 235 million
Muthaiga is one of Nairobi's most prestigious and affluent neighborhoods, known for its exclusivity and serene environment. Properties in Muthaiga are typically expansive, with most sitting on large plots of land, offering residents a luxurious and private living experience. The area is characterized by lush greenery, mature gardens, and proximity to key amenities, including top-notch schools, hospitals, and diplomatic missions.

Karen

KES 160 million
Karen, one of Nairobi's most sought-after neighborhoods, is known for its expansive homes set on large plots, offering both luxury and tranquility. The area is characterized by lush greenery, colonial-era architecture, and proximity to elite schools and shopping centers.

Lavington

KES 70 million
Lavington is a prestigious residential area in Nairobi known for its upscale properties and serene environment. It features a mix of luxury apartments, spacious homes, and modern amenities, appealing to those seeking a high standard of living. The area is characterized by well-maintained streets, green spaces, and proximity to shopping centers and international schools.

Hurlingham

KES 50 million
Hurlingham is an affluent residential area in Nairobi known for its upscale homes and well-maintained properties. It features a mix of modern apartments and elegant houses, often with lush green spaces and high-security measures. The neighborhood is popular among professionals and expatriates due to its proximity to amenities and central location.
